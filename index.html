Before Fear Comes Fear

Most people believe fear begins with anxiety, worry, or panic. Fear Before Fear explores a deeper question. What happens before fear becomes fear? This book journeys beneath the surface of emotion to uncover the assumptions, expectations, imagined futures, and internal stories that quietly shape our lives long before fear becomes visible. Rather than focusing solely on managing fear after it appears, this exploration seeks to understand its hidden beginnings and reveal how fear often starts growing long before we ever recognize its presence.

"Fear often begins long before we feel it."
