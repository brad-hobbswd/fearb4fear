Before Fear Comes Fear

Most people believe fear begins with anxiety, worry, or panic. Fear Before Fear explores a deeper question. What happens before fear becomes fear? This book journeys beneath the surface of emotion to uncover the assumptions, expectations, imagined futures, and internal stories that quietly shape our lives long before fear becomes visible. Rather than focusing solely on managing fear after it appears, this exploration seeks to understand its hidden beginnings and reveal how fear often starts growing long before we ever recognize its presence.

"Fear often begins long before we feel it."

About the Author

Bradley Hobbs is an educator, father, author, and believer in Jesus Christ who is passionate about helping others discover freedom, understanding, and growth. Much of his writing is shaped by a deep curiosity about the questions many people quietly carry through life. The idea for Fear Before Fear grew from a simple question that remained in his mind for years: Is there a way to understand fear before experiencing it? That question sparked a journey of research, reflection, observation, and personal exploration. Through that process, Bradley began examining how fear often develops long before anxiety, worry, or panic ever appear. Writing this book became more than an academic exercise. Putting these ideas into his own words helped him better understand his own relationship with fear and how he could respond to life’s uncertainties in healthier ways. The insights shared throughout these pages were not written to force answers upon others, but to offer thoughtful observations for anyone willing to explore the subject for themselves. His hope is that readers will discover greater awareness, greater freedom, and a deeper understanding of the stories fear often tells before fear is ever felt. After all, fear is a liar.
